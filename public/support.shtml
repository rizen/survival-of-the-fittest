Common Problems and Questions

I can't log in.
Did you create an account? If not, create one.
Do you have cookies turned on in your web browser? If not, enable them.

My character died. Can you restore it for me?
No. This is a game of survival. It's likely your character will die. Check your message log if you want to know why your character died. We do not restore characters.

I think I've found a bug in the game, what do I do?
Post it on the message board. We'll attend to it promptly.

Still Having Problems?

If you're having problems please post them to the message board. We'll be happy to respond to them promptly if they are posted on the board.

We do not provide telephone or email support for Survival of the Fittest. Any phone calls or emails will be disregarded.
